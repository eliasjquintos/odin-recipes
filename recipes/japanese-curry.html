curry

Don't use too much water!

The curry is the seasoning!

Ingredients

  • curry block
  • vegetables/meat
  • water

Steps

  1. Cut the veggies/meat
  2. Lightly cook the meat (and onions)
  3. Add water and bring to a boil
  4. Add veggies and boil
  5. Lower heat to a simmer and stir in the curry block
